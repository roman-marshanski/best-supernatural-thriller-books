## What Qualifies as a Supernatural Thriller?

For the purposes of this list, a supernatural thriller is a book that combines:
- suspense or mystery-driven storytelling  
- elements beyond natural explanation (demons, curses, hidden forces, altered reality)  
- psychological or moral tension  
- consequences that escalate rather than reset

## Are Supernatural Thrillers Also Mystery Books?

Many supernatural thrillers contain strong mystery elements, particularly investigation, hidden truths, and gradual revelation. However, they differ from traditional mystery books by introducing forces that cannot be explained through logic alone.
